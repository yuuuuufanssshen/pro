Carbon Footprint

The amount of greenhouse gases - primarily carbon dioxide - released into the atmosphere by human activity, for example, carbon dioxide emitted when riding a vehicle, using an electrical appliance, what you eat, where it is manufactured, etc.


CO2 Effects on Australia

Carbon footprint contributes to 35% increase in global warming, which may result in glaciers melting and rising temperatures. Thus, many animals lose their habitats and are in peril of extinction
