Why Eco Melbourne?

Eco Melbourne is an educational website, promoting knowledge about carbon footprint and encouraging people to care about the environment. We encourage the users to understand their carbon footprints, its impact on the environment.

We believe each step towards reducing waste including reducing plastic bottles when choosing water fountains to drink water and reducing carbon footprints while choosing to walk or bicycle counts. Small steps like these will make our environment cleaner and greener for a better, brighter and healthy future.
