Why Eco Melbourne?

Eco Melbourne is an informative website, promoting knowledge about carbon footprint and encouraging teenagers to care about the environment. We encourage the users to understand what carbon footprint and its impact on the environment is.

Saving our environment doesn’t require big climate reforms but we can all do our part by making the right environmental choices. Using drinking fountains instead of buying disposable water bottles, walking or riding a bike instead of driving short distances are some small but considerable steps that we can consider to save the planet.

“The future will either be green or not at all” – Bob Brown
